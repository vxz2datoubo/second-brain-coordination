# Research Validation Matrix

| Family | What research can support | What it cannot prove | A-share transfer gate | Stop condition |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Influence diagrams | Explicit decision, chance, information and utility structure | Causal truth or profitable policy | Point-in-time observability and RuleSnapshotRef | Hidden future variable or unresolved causal direction |
| Bayesian updating and proper scores | Coherent updating and calibrated forecast evaluation | Independent evidence or stationary likelihoods | Correlation groups, regime labels and local calibration | No defensible prior/likelihood or duplicated source lineage |
| Value of information | Whether another observation may justify its cost | That obtainable data is timely, legal or decision-changing | Market session, data latency, entitlement and T+1 waiting cost | EVSI below total acquisition and delay cost |
| Robust decision methods | Sensitivity to uncertain distributions and parameters | That the ambiguity set is complete | A-share limits, liquidity and scenario coverage | Recommendation flips across plausible boundary sets |
| Sequential decisions | Structured wait/continue/stop policies | Same-day sellability or frictionless adjustment | Fresh/seasoned inventory and session state | State or transition model not point-in-time valid |
| Portfolio methods | Diversification and constrained allocation mathematics | Stable expected returns/covariances | Lot size, sellability, limit states, capacity and costs | Inputs unstable enough to dominate output |
| Survival and tail risk | Loss concentration and capital survival diagnostics | Exact tail probability from small samples | Limit-down liquidity, suspension and settlement constraints | Stress scenarios or liquidity evidence absent |
| Execution models | Cost/impact decomposition | Fillability without order-book evidence | Auction/session/lot/price rules and executable route | Required queue/event capability unavailable |
| Multiple-testing audit | Inflated discovery risk and trial-family evidence; Romano-Wolf stepdown may control familywise error while using joint dependence | Market truth, future persistence, or replacement for SPA/PBO/DSR/lockbox/cost/capacity gates | Immutable experiment ledger, predeclared alpha, studentized statistics and time-dependence-preserving resampling | Failed variants, trial count, common baseline or defensible resampling scheme missing |
| Regime/decay models | Change detection and conditional instability | A uniquely correct regime label | Rule-effective dates and board/product segmentation | Detection latency makes use non-actionable |
| Attribution | Decomposition under a declared model | Causal credit under omitted factors | Costs, fills, forecast and sizing lineage | Residual or missing data exceeds declared tolerance |

Primary-source bibliography and exact theorem-to-contract mapping remain D1/D2-D4 research deliverables. This D0 matrix is a boundary and validation plan, not a claim that the methods are implemented or A-share validated.
